# NammaExpense v1.1.0 - The Intelligent Update 🧠

We are excited to release the first major version of **NammaExpense**! This release brings powerful, AI-inspired features to help you track your finances smarter and faster.

## ✨ What's New in v1.0
*   **🎙️ Voice Add**: Just tap the mic and say *"100 rupees for petrol"* to add transactions instantly. No typing needed!
*   **📦 Bulk Add Mode**: Busy day? Add 3-5 expenses in one go with our new bulk entry screen.
*   **🧠 Split Brain Insights**: Unlock psychological insights into your spending. Are you a *"Date Night Splurger"* or a *"Morning Coffee Fanatic"*? Find out in the Stats tab!
*   **🕐 Context-Aware Suggestions**: The Quick Add button now smartly suggests categories based on the time of day (Morning, Afternoon, Evening).

## 🚀 Core Features
*   **Smart Tracking**: Log Income, Expenses, and Subscriptions.
*   **Visual Analytics**: Interactive Pie Charts and Heatmaps.
*   **Budget Safety**: Set daily limits and get warned before you overspend.
*   **Privacy First**: 100% Offline storage using SQLite. Your data stays on your device.
*   **Beautiful UI**: Modern Material 3 design with Dark Mode support.
